This exercise is base on the 12th edition of MANAGERIAL - Foundations of Business Analysis and Strategy, chapter 7 - Demand Estimation and Forecasting.

The exercise is about a price-setting firm (the Checkers' Pizza) and uses a multiple regression model. It relates sales, price, competitors' price and household income.

The data is available in the directory in the file 'Data for Checkers Pizza.xlsx'.

The Jupyter Notebook 'demand_estimation_EN.ipynb' contains the exercise, analysis and coments.

There is another Jupyter Notebook in Brazilian Portuguese 'demand_estimation_PT-BR.ipynb'.
